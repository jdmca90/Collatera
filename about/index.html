About Collatera

Collatera began as a personal reference library — a way to keep clinical images, society guidelines, and practical notes gathered during cardiology fellowship in one place I could actually find again. The name nods to collateral circulation: alternate pathways that keep tissue perfused when the primary route is blocked, which felt like a fitting metaphor for something built quietly alongside formal training.

What started as a private study tool is growing into something meant to be shared. The aim is to turn Collatera from a personal reference into a resource hub built for peers — fellows and colleagues who could use the same kind of organized, at-a-glance reference during training and beyond.
